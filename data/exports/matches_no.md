Notater samlet og skrevet av Johhny Corneliussen . Notes of our family by Johnny Corneliussen Traramoen 20 November 1972 Sect Notes Since I was a little boy living on Traramoen... From my boyhood days on the Traramoen I remember that I was nicknamed "Helgesen". An old man raised his the stick to me when we went too far with our antics and said: "Beware you, Helge boy (Hæljesgutten)! Where did this name come from? My father was named Birger Corneliussen, my grandfather, Lars Anton Corenilussen and my great-grandfather Cornelius Andersen. My great-great-grandfather was therefore likely called Anders. And this fits in with what my grandfather told me 27-28 years ago. According to my grandfather, Andres had 5 children: Soren, Cornelius, Nils, Lars and Birthe. Soren went to America. He had 3 sons and 2 daughters. Cornelius, my great-grandfather, had 5 sons and 2 daughters. Nils had a son and 4 daughters. Lars, who lived in Åsebråtveien 1, had a son and a daughter. Birthe married Huth. They went to America and probably had 2 sons and 2 daughters. According to an old document that I got from Ruth (Corenliussen) Mügge my great-great grandfather was named Anders Helgesen. .
